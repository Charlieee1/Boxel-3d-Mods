# Level Editor Overcharged Guide
You will notice extra buttons in the top bar. Hover over them to see what they do.
## Confirm/cancel
Press "c" or click the confirm button to confirm an action.  
Press "v" or click the cancel button to cancel an action.
## Multiselect
Multiselect does not work well with undo/redo.
### Stage 1
Press "4" or click the multiselect button to enable basic multiselect. Move the glowing small blocks around to make your selection. The selected objects will change colour. Press "c" to confirm your selection and move to stage 2.
### Stage 2
Press "2" or confirm your stage 1 selection to enable precise multiselect. Click on blocks to toggle whether they are in the selection or not. Press "c" to confirm your selection and move to stage 3.
### Stage 3
A big translucent block will appear. Move, scale, rotate, duplicate, and delete this block as you would any other block. Press "c" to confirm your modifications and exit multiselect.
## Single direction scaling
Single direction scaling does not work well with undo/redo. It's also kind of janky.  
Select the block you want to scale. Press "y" or click the single direction scaling button to enable the scaling. Move the small control blocks to scale the object. Press "c" to confirm your changes or "v" cancel them.
## Intangibility
With a block selected, press "i" or click the intangibility button to toggle the block's intangibility.
## Select block type
Press "a", followed by one of "`", 0-9, "-", "=", or "\" to select a block type.
## Fast build
Press "k" or click the fast bulid button to enable fast build.  
Once fast build is enabled, use it like so:
1. Click to create a block at your mouse position
2. Click to resize the block to a different mouse position
3. Click to set the rotation of the block
4. Click to set the position of the block
5. Repeat
Press "v" to disable fast build.
## Drag move
Hold "q" and drag blocks around with your mouse. This will only affect its x and y coordinates.
